Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Onions and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients: gather lasagna essentials—noodles, beef, pork, onions, garlic, tomato sauce, mozzarella, Parmesan, basil, oregano, butter, flour, and milk.
  2. Cook Meat Sauce: sauté onions, garlic; brown meat; add tomato sauce, herbs; simmer, possibly deglaze with wine.
  3. Make Béchamel: melt butter, mix flour, add milk gradually; cook until béchamel thickens; season./li>
  4. Layer Lasagna: preheat; layer in greased dish—meat sauce, noodles, béchamel; repeat, topping with mozzarella, Parmesan.
  5. Bake and Serve: Bake till golden, bubbling; rest briefly; serve hot, relishing pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, melted cheese.
