Two minute noodles

Description

Two minute noodles are an ideal meal for the lazy and broke college student.

Ingredients

  • Noodles in cup
  • Flavour packet
  • Water

Steps

  1. Peel the lid halfway
  2. Add flavour packet into the noodles
  3. Pour water into the cup so that the noodles are completely submerged
  4. Micorwave cup for 2 minutes
  5. Eat
